Our plans for Lowfield Green

Our first community share offer raised £420,000, bringing our forever affordable homes a huge step closer.

Our first site at Lowfield Green in York will pioneer an innovative mutual home ownership model that will provide residents with secure equity while guaranteeing permanent affordability.

We have planning approval for nineteen homes and a common house on the site, with our contractor team ready to start building in 2022.
