Our plans for Lowfield Green

We raised over £420,000 through our first Community Share Issue, a huge step towards bringing our first forever affordable homes to York.

Our first site at Lowfield Green will pioneer an innovative mutual home ownership model that will provide residents with secure equity while guaranteeing permanent affordability.

We already have planning approval for nineteen homes and a common house on the site, with our contractor team ready to start building in summer 2020.
