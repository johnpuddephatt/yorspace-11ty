We are York’s community land trust

As a Community Land Trust, we’re a non-profit organisation that owns and develops land for the benefit of the community.

That benefit can come in lots of different ways – from providing affordable homes, to protecting green spaces, to building community services and more.

YorSpace has a track record of generating revenue and accessing grant funding, which when combined with our sector knowledge, will make community led projects come to life.

We are proud to be a member and part of the wider CLT network, which is made up of over 175 organisations across the UK.
